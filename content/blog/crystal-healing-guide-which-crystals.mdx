## How Crystal Healing Works

Crystals are minerals with stable molecular structures that resonate at specific frequencies. When placed near or on the body, this stable resonance interacts with the human energy field. Different crystal compositions produce different frequencies, and these frequencies have characteristic effects on body and mind.

The traditions that work with crystals include Vedic gemstone therapy (which uses specific gemstones to strengthen specific planets), Tibetan and Chinese energy traditions, modern Western crystal healing, and contemporary integrative practices.

While scientific research on crystal healing is limited, the consistency of reports across cultures and centuries suggests the phenomenon is real even if the mechanism is not yet fully understood.

## Crystals for Anxiety and Mental Calm

**Amethyst:** The most widely recommended crystal for anxiety. Amethyst calms mental activity, supports meditation, and is particularly helpful for sleep difficulty driven by mental restlessness. Carry a piece in the pocket during the day, place beside the bed at night.

**Rose Quartz:** Works on the heart center. Particularly helpful for anxiety rooted in emotional patterns: grief, fear of abandonment, anxiety about relationships. Hold over the heart during meditation.

**Lepidolite:** Contains lithium (the same element used in psychiatric medication for mood regulation). Particularly supportive for nervous system regulation and chronic anxiety patterns. Wear as a pendant or hold during anxious moments.

**Sodalite:** Calms mental over-activity. Useful when the mind is racing with thoughts.

**Howlite:** Supports patience and tolerance, helpful when anxiety expresses as irritability.

## Crystals for Sleep

**Amethyst:** As above. Particularly when sleep difficulty involves mental rumination.

**Moonstone:** Works with the Moon's rhythm and supports natural sleep cycles. Place under the pillow or beside the bed.

**Selenite:** Cleanses energy and supports clear, restful sleep. Place at the head of the bed.

**Pearl (Moti):** Vedic gemstone for the Moon. Supports emotional regulation and sleep. Worn as a ring on the little finger of the right hand after chart-based consultation.

## Crystals for Relationships and Love

## How to Use Crystals Daily

**As jewelry:** Pendant near the heart, ring on appropriate finger, earrings, bracelet. The most direct contact.

**In the pocket:** A small tumbled stone carried in the pocket provides ongoing energetic support.

**At the workspace:** Larger crystals (clusters, geodes) at the desk balance work energy. Citrine and Clear Quartz are common.

**In the bedroom:** Amethyst or Selenite at the bedside supports sleep. Rose Quartz under the pillow supports emotional rest.

**In meditation:** Hold the relevant crystal during meditation, or place at the third eye, heart, or crown.

## Cleansing and Charging

Crystals absorb energy and need periodic cleansing:

- Running water (avoid for soft stones like Selenite, Calcite)
- Sunlight (avoid for crystals that fade: Rose Quartz, Amethyst)
- Moonlight (universally safe, particularly on full Moon nights)
- Smudging with sage, sandalwood, or palo santo incense
- Burial in soil for several hours
- Sound (singing bowls, mantras)

Charging is often done on full Moon nights by leaving the crystal in moonlight overnight.
