## Why Sade Sati Matters

Sade Sati is the most misunderstood transit in Vedic astrology. Popular forecasts treat it as a guaranteed period of suffering. Classical Jyotish treats it as a karmic examination, Saturn's slow, deliberate test of where a life has been built honestly and where it has not. The difference is not academic. Fear-based readings produce panic. Wisdom-based readings produce growth.
