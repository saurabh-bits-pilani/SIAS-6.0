Astro Vastu integrates two ancient Vedic sciences: astrology (the analysis of how planets influence individual life) and Vastu Shastra (the architectural science of how directional energy affects a building's inhabitants). When combined, the two systems produce far more targeted recommendations than either alone. This guide explains the planetary basis of Vastu, common home corrections, and how an Astro Vastu consultant integrates the two for personalized recommendations.

## What is Astro Vastu?

Vastu Shastra is the classical Indian science of architecture that maps the energetic properties of directions, materials, and building positions. The premise is that every space has an energetic structure determined by its orientation, layout, materials, and the directional placement of functions (kitchen, bedroom, prayer room, etc.).

Vedic astrology adds the dimension of the resident's birth chart. The same Vastu structure that supports one person may not support another, because each native has different planetary strengths and weaknesses. The astro vastu consultant therefore reads both:

- The home's Vastu structure (which directions house which functions)
- The resident's birth chart (which planets are strong, which are weak, what life themes are active)

The recommendations are then tailored: corrections that strengthen the resident's weak planets in their corresponding directions, and corrections that allow the resident's strong planets to express through their directions.

## How Vastu Directions Relate to Planets

The classical correspondence between directions and planets:

| Direction | Planet | Element | Themes |
|---|---|---|---|
| North | Mercury (Budh) | Water | Wealth, business, learning |
| Northeast (Ishaan) | Jupiter (Brihaspati) | Water/Air | Wisdom, spirituality, growth |
| East | Sun (Surya) | Fire | Vitality, authority, father |
| Southeast (Agneya) | Venus (Shukra) | Fire | Beauty, comfort, kitchen fires |
| South | Mars (Mangal) | Fire/Earth | Strength, ambition, victory |
| Southwest (Nairutya) | Rahu (or Earth/Saturn) | Earth | Stability, ancestors, weight |
| West | Saturn (Shani) | Air | Profession, sustenance, longevity |
| Northwest (Vayavya) | Moon (Chandra) | Air | Movement, communication, guests |
| Center (Brahmasthan) | Brahma | Akasha (space) | Open space, family unity |

Each direction's corresponding planet rules the activities placed there. A kitchen in the Southeast (Venus) is favored because Venus rules sensory pleasure and food. A prayer room in the Northeast (Jupiter) is favored because Jupiter rules dharma and learning.

## Common Home Vastu Principles

**Main door:** Best in East (Sun) or North (Mercury). These directions invite positive energy. Southwest entrance is the most challenging and should be reserved for guard houses or service entrances.

**Kitchen:** Best in Southeast (Venus's fire). Northeast kitchens cause significant Vastu doshas because Jupiter's wisdom direction is then occupied by Venus's fire activity.

**Bedroom (master):** Best in Southwest (Earth's stability). Sleep with head toward South or East, never toward North. The North-head sleeping position is energetically considered the most depleting.

**Prayer room (puja room):** Best in Northeast (Jupiter). Should be clean, open, and elevated if possible.

**Bathroom and toilet:** Best in Northwest (Moon) or West (Saturn). Should never be in the Northeast.

**Heavy items and storage:** Best in Southwest (Earth's weight). Heavy furniture, safes, and storage should anchor the Southwest.

**Open space:** Most needed in Northeast (Jupiter's freedom) and Center (Brahmasthan).

## How Birth Chart Modifies Vastu Recommendations

A native with weak Saturn in their birth chart may experience particular benefit from strengthening the West (Saturn's direction) in their home. The astro vastu consultant might recommend:

- A Saturn yantra in the West
- Specific colors associated with Saturn (deep blue, black) in the West
- Western windows that bring afternoon light
- Career-related items (computer, professional documents) housed in the West

For a native with weak Jupiter, similar attention goes to the Northeast.

This personalization is the key difference between generic Vastu (which gives universal rules) and Astro Vastu (which tunes the rules to the resident's chart).

## Vastu for the North East Direction (Ishaan)

The Northeast direction (Ishaan) deserves special attention because of its classical importance:

- Ruled by Jupiter, the great benefic
- Element is water and air (lightness)
- Themes: wisdom, growth, spiritual development, family wealth, child welfare

Northeast should:

- Remain open and uncluttered
- Have water features (small fountain, water aquarium) if possible
- Be used for prayer room, study room, or simply open space
- Receive natural light
- Be slightly lower in floor height than the rest of the house if possible

Northeast should NOT have:

- Heavy storage
- Kitchen or fire-based activities
- Toilet or sewage
- Master bedroom
- Heavy furniture

When Northeast doshas exist (kitchen, toilet, heavy storage in Northeast), Astro Vastu corrections include: copper pyramids at specific points, water bowls or aquariums to restore the water element, color corrections, and yantras to redirect energy. Structural change is recommended only when other corrections do not suffice.

## Common Vastu Doshas and Their Remedies

**Northeast cut or extension reduction:** Remedied with brass or copper pyramids placed at the corner, sometimes with crystals.

**Southwest opening (door or large window in Southwest):** Remedied with mirrors, heavy curtains, and Earth element items (stone, ceramic).

**Toilet in Northeast:** Severe dosha. Remedied with sea salt bowls, neem placements, periodic energy clearing rituals, and ideally relocation if possible.

**Kitchen in Northeast:** Severe dosha. Remedied as above, with additional attention to Jupiter and Venus yantras to balance the directional confusion.

**South or West open spaces (rather than heavy):** Remedied by adding furniture or heavy decorative elements.

**Main door in Southwest:** Significant dosha. Remedied with multi-layered correction including pyramids, mirrors, and ritual energization.
